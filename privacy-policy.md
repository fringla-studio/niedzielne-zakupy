# Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

The **Sunday Shopping in Poland** app is designed to work without user accounts and without asking for personal information.

## What data do we collect?

The app does not require registration, login, or personal data.

We only collect anonymous app usage statistics, such as information about which screens and features are used. This helps us improve the app, fix issues, and understand which parts of the app are most useful.

We use **TelemetryDeck** for these anonymous analytics.

## What we do not do

The app:

* does not show ads,
* does not create user accounts,
* does not sell data,
* does not use analytics data for marketing purposes,
* does not track users across apps or websites.

## Analytics data

Analytics data is anonymous and used only to improve the app. We do not use it to identify individual users.

TelemetryDeck may process an anonymous app installation identifier, app usage events, an approximate event time, and basic technical information such as the app version, operating system version, or device type.

## Contact

For privacy-related questions, you can contact us through the app’s public GitHub repository or through the contact details provided on the developer website.
